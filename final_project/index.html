About Us

Scoots is a premier motor scooter rental company nestled in the heart of Cozumel, an idyllic island off the coast of Mexico in the Western Caribbean Sea. As an owner-operated business, Scoots prides itself on serving a diverse clientele, including locals, walk-in tourists, motor scooter aficionados, and cruise-based touring groups.

With two strategic locations—one near the bustling Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in El Centro and the other close to the Terminal Puerta Maya, catering to cruise line passengers—Scoots offers convenient access to top-quality motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs for exploring Cozumel's enchanting landscapes.

Our mission extends beyond simple rentals; we aim to embody the island's spirit of adventure and freedom, inviting customers to explore Cozumel's lesser-known coasts and inlets far from the typical tourist trails. Our website serves as a comprehensive portal for savvy travelers planning their island escapades, providing essential information on our services, rental requirements, and the booking process, all while capturing the carefree and exhilarating essence of Cozumel. At Scoots, we're not just about getting you a ride; we're about crafting unforgettable experiences.
