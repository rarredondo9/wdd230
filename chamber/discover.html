Attractions

Parque Ecológico Cayalá

An ecological and sports park in Guatemala, offering a natural environment where visitors can engage with a variety of flora and fauna, including over 117 bird species. It hosts events, educational programs, and volunteer opportunities, emphasizing environmental conservation and outdoor activities. The park operates with a donation-based entry fee and includes attractions like guided tours and a bird museum.Learn more...

Ciudad Cayalá

A high-level real estate project designed by architect Leon Krier, featuring a mix of offices, homes, retail spaces, parking, dining options, academies, gyms, cinemas, banks, medical centers, and an embassy, all in one location. It has a rich history dating back to 1913 and focuses on sustainability through its Cayalá ECO-SOCIAL division.Learn more...

Parque Ecológico Kanajuyú

A tranquil escape offering various outdoor activities such as walking paths, picnic areas, and spaces for sports. It emphasizes ecological conservation and provides a green, peaceful environment within the urban area for relaxation and recreation.Learn more...

El Pulté Golf

An exclusive residential community designed for family life, nature, and security, featuring a top-level golf course as its centerpiece. Surrounded by unparalleled views, it offers a privileged living environment with five-star amenities and comfort. The golf course, designed by Dye Designs, is one of the best private courses in Latin America, with 18 holes and a par 72 layout. It's a community where families can live in harmony with nature.Learn more...

Fundacion Rozas-Botrán

With roots dating back to 1915 in Quetzaltenango, is dedicated to "The Art of Helping" through healthcare, attention to the destitute, and cultural development via the visual arts. It focuses on scientific research for treating metabolic and genetic diseases and manages artistic projects to transform lives. Their vision is to be a world-class leader in social development projects in health and culture for Central America and Panama. Learn more...

Le Petit Paris

Guatemala's premier French dessert boutique, renowned for its high-quality, fresh, and innovatively varied products. It offers an authentic Parisian experience, making genuine French desserts accessible to Latin American customers.Learn more...
